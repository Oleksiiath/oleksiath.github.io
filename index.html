Welcome to the EngUnique

EngUnique is the Ukrainian language company focusing on teaching students to improve their knowledge in English. The aims of company consist of preparation for international language exams, as well as Cambridge and Oxford ones, and others. Students can can give it a try while taking a mock exam for free initially, as long as the participation in any course has been verified. Besides, the company EngUnique pays considerable attention to studying children. Thereby, we have created special school for them, where cockamamie ideas are being realized during each lesson. In addition, EngUnique has corporative studying for different companies with decent discount. Noteworthy workshops, daily digests, speaking clubs, vebinars, conferences - everything is awaiting you here and now. Futhermore, our company sets off breathtaking tours for all of you. For best ones the considerable discount will be beyond a shadow of a doubt! Thereby, EngUnique movivates you to learn English better and keep it up, and gives you mind-blowing opportunity to visit different places of the globe, you just cannot pass up! If you want to see our travelling experiences, welcome here With no innuendo to show off, our teachers are by far experienced professionals, having taken either IELTS exam or Cambridge ones very well. Become a part of our academy and you'll never regret about it! By the way, three rules of ours we strictly stick to: everyone is gifted enought to speak English fluently, there are not stupid questions, English for everyone. Join us!
